Component specs · Brief 1

Product Card

One card, three containers, six states. The card is the workhorse of the catalog — it appears on /catalog, /guide, every reorder tile, every category strip. This spec resolves the audit findings: color drift, the FLF-logo fallback, retina sizing, in-cart invisibility, and producer attribution.

Color rule

Brand-blue everywhere for routine actions — Add, qty stepper, primary CTAs. Green is for the commit moment only (Place order, Confirm). The stepper renders blue on /cart, /guide, /catalog — same component, no per-screen overrides.

No-photo fallback

Not the FLF logo. Each fallback is a typographic monogram of the producer's initials on --brand-blue-tint, dot-grid background, with the category in an eyebrow caps line and the farm name underneath. Treats the absent photo as editorial space, not a missing asset.

In-cart visibility

A persistent blue pill × N sits at top-right of the media — visible at rest, while scrolling, and on every container. The qty stepper is no longer the only signal. On guide rows where there's no media corner, the count moves into the stepper's val.

Guide indicator

Replaces the unlabeled gold star with a small badge — gold star + "In guide" text — on the media. On guide rows the redundant photo badge drops; a tiny gold "In guide" flag sits beside the producer line instead.

Producer byline

Two lines, structured. PRODUCER in eyebrow caps above the product name (machine-readable, for filters). "From Hartke's flock" in italic below — the editorial nod, in brand voice. Falls off in row layout where horizontal space is tight; eyebrow stays.

Retina sizing

Compact grid: 1× 280, 2× 560, 3× 840. Guide row: 1× 80, 2× 160, 3× 240. Reorder tile: 1× 220, 2× 440, 3× 660. PDP hero: 1× 720, 2× 1440. Ship srcset + sizes; never upscale a single <img>.

04

Interaction states — compact

demoed on variant 01; rules apply to all

Hover deepens the hairline (no shadow, no lift). Focus-visible draws a 2px brand-blue ring offset 2px — same ring on the card root for keyboard catalog browsing and on the Add button. Active presses the Add button to 98% scale and shifts to --brand-blue-dark. All three live on the same easing — cubic-bezier(.2,.8,.2,1), 150ms.
