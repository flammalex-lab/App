Component specs · Brief 2

Mobile chrome

Buyers are restaurant owners placing $300+ orders one-handed from a hot kitchen. Today's chrome eats 12% of the viewport before any product paints, and the cart lives in the worst thumb position. This spec collapses the header, promotes Cart to a tab, and stops the floating bar from fighting with toasts and the home indicator.

52px header (down from 96px)

One bar carries brand mark, page identity, cutoff, and cart access. Wordmark drops on mobile; the circular mark + page title carry brand recognition. Cutoff lives where the date strip used to — same information, half the height.

Cart is now a tab

5 tabs: Guide / Catalog / Cart / Standing / Account. The badge count lives on the Cart tab — visible from any screen, in thumb reach. Top-right cart icon is removed. aria-current="page" on the active tab.

StickyCartBar transitions

Visible when cart ≥ 1 item and active tab ≠ Cart. Hides on the Cart screen (the tab is enough). Pinned bottom: calc(83px + 10px) above the tab bar; toasts move to bottom: calc(83px + 70px) when sticky is up.

Stepper unifies on blue

One QtyStepper — brand-blue outline at rest, filled blue when count ≥ 1. The green variant on /guide is deleted. Green is reserved for the commit moment.

Safe-area

Tab bar uses padding-bottom: max(8px, env(safe-area-inset-bottom)). StickyCartBar is positioned above the tab bar, so the home indicator only ever touches the tab bar's padding region — never live UI.

Active-tab treatment

3px brand-blue underline anchored to the top of the tab cell, icon + label switch to brand-blue. No background swatch — keeps the floor visually quiet. Reduced motion: instant; otherwise the underline animates 150ms via --ease-fluent.

What changed · annotations

Mark + title fuse

The wordmark drops on mobile. The 28px logo paired with a Bricolage page title carries identity in 1/3 the height.

Cutoff is sub-titular

Lives as the tiny line under the page title. Pulse dot turns red inside the last 60 min. Tappable: opens delivery schedule sheet.

Right side carries only utility

Search + notifications. Cart leaves the header — it has its own tab.

Sticky bar sits in the gap

10px above the tab bar, 12px from screen edges. The tab bar's own safe-area padding absorbs the home indicator below.
